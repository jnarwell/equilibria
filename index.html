EQUILIBRIA

sixteen live systems · play · breed · couple

Can a machine make art? Not by itself. But describe a process — a slime mold, a flock, a field of coupled oscillators — and an AI can build it in seconds, then hand you the instrument to play it.

Sixteen self-organizing systems, none of them pre-drawn — every frame is computed live from a local rule. This isn't AI generating a picture. It's AI building the tools, and a human deciding what to do with them. The art isn't the output; it's the performance.
